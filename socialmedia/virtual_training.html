Digital and Social

World-Class Training, Virtually

Dannette Veale - January 18, 2010 - 5 Comments

On Tuesday, January 26 Cisco Live and Networkers Virtual will host a full day of world-class training, keynote presentations, live executive chat session, and a virtual World of Solutions with live virtual staff. This is a great opportunity for you to participate in an exciting event without leaving your desk.

During this one-day live-virtual hybrid event taking place in parallel with Networkers at Cisco Live Barcelona you will hear compelling keynotes from two technology visionaries streamed live in the Cisco Live and Networkers Virtual Auditorium.  Marthin De Beer, Senior Vice President, Emerging Technologies Group delivers the Cisco keynote.  Bruce Thomas, Director of the Wearable Computer Laboratory, at the University of South Australia, will share his knowledge and vision of wearable computers, augmented reality and virtual reality. There will also be several live technical sessions for attendees to view, see below.

ITMCOC-2901: Cisco on Cisco – Transformational Business Value – Data Center
ITMPCS-2901: Virtualization and Networking
BRKCRS-2901: High Density Client Design Principles in Wireless LAN Deployments*
BRKDCT-2901: Securing a Wireless Lan Controller as a Network Device in your Data Center*
BRKEWN-2901: WLC Config Analyzer Tutorial*
BRKEWN-2902: Borderless Mobility: Cisco Unified Wireless Network – Systems Architecture*
BRKNMS-2901: A New Paradigm for Element Management Systems*
BRKSEC-2901: Borderless Network Security Architecture*
BRKSPG-2901: Mobile Ad-Hoc Networks*
BRKSPV-2901: Video Surveillance over Mesh Design and Deployment Best Practices*
BRKUCC-2901: Unified Communications Integration for IBM Sametime*
(*These sessions require Premier access to view and are not available to Guest access attendees.)

Take advantage of all the great content Cisco Live and Networks Virtual has to offer.  View the keynote addresses, join the post-keynote chat session with Marthin De Beer, network with your peers in the lounge, and learn from the expansive selection of technical sessions available.

Don’t miss this opportunity to hear the latest information about where Cisco is taking IT and communications. Join us on Tuesday, January 26, for a full day of comprehensive learning and networking opportunities. Login at www.ciscolivevirtual.com.
