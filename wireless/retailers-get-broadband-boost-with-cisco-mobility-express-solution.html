Retailers Get Broadband Boost with Cisco Mobility Express Solution

Bill Rubino - September 22, 2015 - 6 Comments

Access to reliable and secure Wi-Fi is no longer a luxury in the retail world; it’s a necessity. Whether you’re looking to add a network or simply upgrade an older one, Cisco’s Mobility+Express&COUNTRY_SITE=us&POSITION=social+media+organic&REFERRING_SITE=Blog&CREATIVE=mobility" target="_blank">Mobility Express Solution makes it easy to provide customers with Wi-Fi, add security cameras and even introduce wireless POS terminals to expedite checkouts. And when you add Cisco Connected Mobile Experiences to your deployment, you can capture presence analytics and engage with your customer through the wi-fi network.

Overloaded networks stop shopping, security and inventory checks in their tracks.

Got ten minutes? Then you can have a connected network. Add another 20 minutes and you can have a Connected Mobile Experiences presence analytics deployment and captive portal.

Simple, quick and seamless.

Support security cameras, speedy mobile checkouts, enhanced customer shopping experiences and more – with minimal IT support – with Mobility Express Solution. Understand and engage your customers better with the added capabilities of Connected Mobile Experiences.
