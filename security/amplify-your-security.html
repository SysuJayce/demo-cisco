      Hi Hasan, AMP for Endpoints is a next generation endpoint security solution, meaning that we provide capabilities to not only PREVENT attacks, but also rapidly DETECT and RESPOND to any malicious behavior found on endpoints. One of our many prevention capabilities includes a built-in AV detection engine.
