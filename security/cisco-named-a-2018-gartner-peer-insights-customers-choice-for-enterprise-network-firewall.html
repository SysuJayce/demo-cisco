Cisco Named a 2018 Gartner Peer Insights Customers’ Choice for Enterprise Network Firewall

Susan Runowicz-Smith - February 13, 2018 - 0 Comments

Cisco has been honored by its users to be named a 2018 Gartner Peer Insights Customers’ Choice for Enterprise Network Firewall.  The Gartner Peer Insights Customers’ Choice distinctions are a ranking of vendors by verified end-user professionals, taking into account both the number of end-user reviews, and the overall user ratings. We are thrilled to be recognized as a trusted security supplier and adviser to our customers in an increasingly difficult cybersecurity environment.

Cisco garnered praise from both new and long-standing customers across a wide range of industries, commending Cisco’s proven and reliable platform that comes with a vast and active user community and support network. Users were in agreement on reasons for choosing Cisco’s solution. They praised Cisco’s ease of implementation, integration of multi-layered security features into a single platform, flexible management options, innovative security automation, great performance, and low operational costs among others.

Here are some excerpts from user reviews posted in the past year that contributed to the distinction.

“Professional implementation, administration is quite easy…All in all, I am very satisfied as the appliance is solid and has never given us a single issue.” — CIO in the Services Industry https://www.gartner.com/reviews/review/view/424302

“It’s Almost Like A Standard Requirement. Mature technology and product”—Sr. IT Director,  Retail Industry  https://www.gartner.com/reviews/review/view/423764

“Now I See….We previously used the IPS sensors with our ASA’s. We were like an ostrich with its head in the ground. This has changed that outlook. We now have visibility into active threats. We see attacks and where they are sourcing from in real-time.” —Network Engineer, Manufacturing Industry https://www.gartner.com/reviews/review/view/422927

“More than perfect network security is exactly what we needed. Proven and reliable are only two descriptive words to describe how it meet our demands.”— Ambassador in the Education Industry https://www.gartner.com/reviews/review/view/421675

“Very solid product. ….We are solving the constantly-evolving problem of keeping client info secure and still allow individuals within the agency to access this information as it is required for use. Currently, we access files from desktops, laptops, and iPads, and Cisco ASA has worked perfectly on all of them.”— Operations Director,  Education https://www.gartner.com/reviews/review/view/415524

Cisco Systems, Inc. received a Gartner Peer Insights Customer Choice Distinction in three categories. Click here to read about each of these distinctions: Enterprise Mobility Management Suites (EMM), Enterprise Network Firewall (ENFW) and Meeting Solutions.

Gartner Peer Insights reviews constitute the subjective opinions of individual end-users based on their own experiences, and do not represent the views of Gartner or its affiliates.

Gartner Peer Insights Customers’ Choice distinctions are determined by the subjective opinions of individual end-user customers based on their own experiences, the number of published reviews on Gartner Peer Insights and overall ratings for a given vendor in the market, as further described here and are not intended in any way to represent the views of Gartner or its affiliates.
