Cisco Live UK 2012 Industrial Sector: Get a head start solving business needs at the Industrial Solutions Booth

Peter Granger - January 9, 2012 - 0 Comments

Cisco Live UK 2012 Banner" src="https://alln-extcloud-storage.cisco.com/ciscoblogs/Cisco-Live-UK-2012-Banner-300x119.jpg" alt="" width="344" height="126" />

Cisco Live UK (January 30th-Feb 2nd in London)  is right around the corner.  A good number of our attendees hail from the industrial verticals such as Transportation, Oil & Gas, Water/Waste Water Treatment, Automotive, and Food & Beverage and Consumer Packaged Goods.

These industrial companies are wrestling with business challenges such as reducing costs, speeding time to market and improving production uptime. How does Cisco play in the industrial space you ask? What solutions do we provide for positively transforming operational plant networking environments?

Well, we learn from what you asked us last year to have our very own Cisco Industrial Solutions booth to give customers and partners a hands-on and in-depth opportunity to see for themselves what we have to offer. Check out the video above. 

Learn Best Practices for Your Production Environment

In summary, we will be showcasing Cisco’s industrial capabilities and showing the wireless, security, and network convergence we are bringing to the production environment.  For companies who deal with industrial networking issues or are curious on how the latest technology can provide operational network benefits, the Industrial Solutions booth in the World of Solutions Showcase provides the perfect opportunity to discuss their needs & requirements with Cisco’s industrial networking team specialist. Customers can learn best practices in areas such as:

  • Secure remote access & protection against cyber threats
  • Energy monitoring & optimisation
  • Remote telemetry & predictive condition based monitoring
  • Event response & coordination
  • Asset tracking & tracing
  • Running multiple services (data, voice, video) over a converged industrial network

Industrial Networking, Wireless, Data Management for the Factory and More

The demonstrations in the Industrial Solutions Booth cover four main areas:

  1. Converged Industrial Ethernet
  2. Converged Industrial Wireless
  3. Industrial Data Management and Workforce Enablement and finally
  4. Industrial Machine to Machine.

All of these areas are critical to the production environment of a typical manufacturer and have applicability to other industrial needs.

The video from last year’s show gives you a glimpse into some of the demonstrations you can expect.  Andy Manuel from our Industrial Automation, Borderless Networks group explains the convergence of video, security and collaboration solutions for the production environment.

Stay tuned for more information in upcoming blogs, particularly on our demos at Cisco Live UK and our Industrial Solutions presence. Thanks for reading.
