Cisco I-Prize Update and some questions

David Hsieh - May 27, 2008 - 15 Comments

Just a quick update: We’re now solidly in the final phase of the Cisco I-Prize">Cisco I-Prize. There are 12 finalist teams from 10 countries on 5 continents vying for to be the winning team. Each team met face to face with the judging panel via TelePresence">Cisco TelePresence for their final presentation and the judging team is deliberating. The quality, thoughtfulness and passion of the final presentations surprised all of us! We’ll announce the winning in team in June, so stay tuned.In the meantime, I’d like your thoughts on 2 topics: 1) Should we continue the Cisco I-Prize">Cisco I-Prize competition again (“Season 2”)? If so, what should we change or improve? 2) We’re going to morph this blog into a discussion on innovation, not necessarily specific to the Cisco I-Prize. What innovation-related topics would like to hear from Cisco on?Let us know!
