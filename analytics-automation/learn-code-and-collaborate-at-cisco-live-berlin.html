Analytics & Automation

Learn, Code, and Collaborate at Cisco Live Berlin!

Gino Palozzi - January 28, 2016 - 0 Comments

Cisco Live Berlin! February 15-19, 2016

Calling all developers, architects, and solution providers—Cisco Live Berlin features the DevNet Zone designed to help you navigate today’s changing application and architecture landscape.

Of particular interest will be the focus on analytics and automation software. As organizations struggle with digital disruption, attention will be drawn to how developers can lead the transformation required by helping:

  • Access and aggregate data from everywhere and analyze it anywhere to unlock new insights and enable timely informed decisions.
  • Interconnect widely distributed systems of apps, data , and devices to streamline and automate services and processes across your organization and growing partner and vendor ecosystems
  • Develop engaging applications that can be easily integrated with your existing apps using our SDKs, including mobile solutions that enable location-aware services and developer.cisco.com/site/cisco-instant-connect/discover/overview/">push-to-talk on-prem communications.

Join us for hands-on experience and face-to-face support from our developer teams in the DevNet Zone. We have many in-depth sessions about our vast Analytics and Automation software portfolio, including:

 

API and App Ecosystems – Build The Best

At Cisco, we have a unique perspective on how to achieve full life-cycle management and governance of your API’s. This includes defining, building, publishing, supporting, and retiring API’s with an enterprise-class ecosystem to better address business outcomes and provide consistency across your portfolio.

Tuesday, Feb 16, 2:00 p.m.  Reserve Your Spot

Enterprise Application to Infrastructure Integration with SDN Apps

What if you could integrate your apps and infrastructure to create truly business drive SDNs? Attend this session to learn how you can connect enterprise applications (EA) and you networking stack (NS) by using the Cisco Automation and Integration Platform in order to automate the management of data and traffic to accelerate application delivery.

Thursday, Feb 18, 9:00 a.m. Reserve Your Spot

Create B2B Exchanges with Cisco Connected Processes

To ensure business continuity and optimization, organizations are automating many critical workflows and infrastructure operations throughout their enterprise and extended ecosystems. With Cisco Connected Processes software, you can deliver business processes and automation as a service, while managing workflows and data more efficiently and effectively. Learn how scalable operational efficiencies can save you time and money while simplifying collaboration between all the members of your technical community.

Thursday, Feb 18, 12:30 p.m. Reserve Your Spot

Profiting from the Next Phase of the Mobility Revolution

Deliver personalized experiences through mobile applications that engage people in real-time based on location. Attend this session to learn how you can quickly develop new mobile experiences and integrate them easily with underlying network and location services. You can quickly create, deploy, and manage context-aware mobile experiences, while using data, such as location and user-profile information, to engage people on their mobile devices and deliver differentiated services while also implementing new business models

Thursday, Feb 18, 3:30pm Reserve Your Spot

New Ways to Engage Customers on Kiosks and Interactive Displays

According to Gartner, 89% of companies expect to compete mostly on the basis of customer experience. A new approach to kiosks and digital displays is allowing companies to offer differentiated services with engaging experiences. In this session, you’ll learn how you can use the Cisco Interactive Experience Platform to deploy interactive, engaging content at the right time, anywhere.

Tuesday, Feb 16, 10:30 a.m.  Reserve Your Spot

Cisco Instant Connect Express

Cisco Instant Connect Express is designed to enable quick, rapid-deploy dispatch networks and command centers for first responders and field workers. Attend this session to learn how you can combine existing emergency communications (traditional and digital mobile radio, cell, Wi-Fi, IP, etc.) into a single, cost-effective system for smaller dispatch units as well as Sonim-hardened Android devices.

Tuesday, Feb 16, 5:00 p.m. Reserve Your Spot

Space fills up fast for the analytics and automation sessions, so make sure you register soon. Not yet registered for Cisco Live? Need help making the case for going to the conference? Check out some of the many compelling reasons to share. We hope to see you in the DevNet zone at Cisco Live Berlin!

See more information about Cisco’s Analytics & Automation portfolio.
