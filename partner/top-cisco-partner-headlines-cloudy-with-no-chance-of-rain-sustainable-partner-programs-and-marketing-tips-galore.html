Top Cisco Partner Headlines: Cloudy with No Chance of Rain, Sustainable Partner Programs, and Marketing Tips Galore

Alexandra Krasne - March 13, 2012 - 0 Comments

“Take risks—no one is going to die!”

This was just one piece of business wisdom we heard from Jeffrey Hayzlett, former Chief Marketing Officer at Kodak and author of the book Running the Gauntlet.

Jeffrey spoke at Partner Velocity, Cisco’s biggest partner marketing event of the year. We’ll share more words of wisdom, interviews, and tips we recently brought home from the event in this episode of Partner Update.

We’ll also cover a number of cloud-related stories, including cloud marketing resources, Light Reading’s Cloud Mega Test results, the Cloud Partner program, and how to ease cloud deployment of the Microsoft Private Cloud solutions" target="_blank" class="seoal-link">Private Cloud solutions. We’ve also got a host of new partner resources and templates, plus our Tweet of the Week.

Have five minutes to spare? Tune in now and get all your Cisco partner news.

Need more info? Keep reading for links, descriptions, and details on each item covered in our newscast.

0:34:  Boost Sales! Leverage the Cisco Cloud Marketing Kit
The recently unveiled Cisco Cloud Marketing Kit is a free resource designed for partners to position cloud-based solutions to customers and prospects. It’s your No. 1 grab-bag for everything cloud, including campaign and program links, collateral, presentations, sales tools, and more.

0:54:  The Proof is in the Cloud
Do your customers need some ironclad cloud proof points? Check out Light Reading’s Cloud Mega Test results. Third-party firm EANTC conducted Cisco CloudVerse testing affirming that, “Cisco has all the components one would need to offer cloud services.” You can read the full test report here.

1:15:  CPS for Cloud: Your Resource for Differentiation
Another cloud resource now available is Collaborative Professional Services (CPS) for Cloud. Whether you’re planning, building, or running clouds, CPS for Cloud provides you with the tools and services you need to develop differentiated cloud solutions. So check it out and learn how to increase your wallet share! [I need to add link for CPS for Cloud ]To leverage CPS for Cloud, partners need to be part of Cisco’s Cloud Builder track in the Cloud Partner program. If you’re not part of the Cloud Partner program, no worries. Learn more about the Cloud Builder track and how to become a member of Cisco’s Cloud Partner Program.

1:58:  Ease Deployment of the Microsoft Private Cloud Solutions
Want the lowdown on the new FlexPod solution with Microsoft Private Cloud? I recently chatted with Rick Snyder, Cisco VP of Global Strategic Partner Organization, to get the details for you. This new release helps to ease deployments of the Microsoft Private Cloud solutions built on our shared infrastructure with NetApp. Also, FlexPods can now be purchased pre-integrated for our US and Canada Channel partners through our work with Avnet. Head here to learn more about this new solution.

2:30:  Take Advantage of Templates/Q&As to Expand Your Business Reach
Collaboration. Borderless Networks. Data Center/Virtualization. Service Provider. Ready to go full throttle into any of these space? Great! Take advantage of Steps to Success, a site for partners that currently features 47 proposal templates and 19 Q&A documents. Be sure to bookmark this site as updates and new proposals are continuously being added—21 new proposals will be added this month alone.

2:52:  Partner Velocity: Talk About a Marketing Mash-Up!
Partner Velocity was a huge success, and we loved meeting with partners and understanding their social media needs. There was so much to cover, so we split our update into two parts: We cover part two in this episode of Partner Update. Be sure to also watch part one, which we filmed onsite in Vegas.

Missed Partner Velocity? Not to worry. We met with speakers to get tips and key takeaways from their presentations. We also chatted with industry analysts who gave us some perspective on partner marketing, Cisco’s VP of Partner Marketing Amanda Jobbins, and talked to Cisco partners who told us what key learnings they’d be implementing right away.

You can find all of the interviews on our YouTube channel. Look for the Partner Velocity link under the Partner tab. We’ll be adding videos over the coming weeks, including interviews with Velocity speakers and industry experts Chris Brogan, Jeffrey Hayzlett, Sally Hogshead, Peter Hinnson, and more. So be sure to check back.

And if you want to watch the full replays of all the speakers, head to the Partner Velocity site next week. The replays will be available in the Auditorium of the virtual environment.

4:36: A Tweet to Die For!
Our top Tweet comes from @Jptello…

  • “Louisville to Vegas & back: 4 mullets, 11 cowboy hats, 2 heel blisters, 200 emails, 5 inspiring #velocitylv panelists, 10 action items.”

We’ll have more Cisco news to share with you in a couple of weeks. Until then, you stay classy partners!
