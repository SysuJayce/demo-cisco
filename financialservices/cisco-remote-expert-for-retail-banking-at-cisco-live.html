Cisco Remote Expert for Retail Banking at Cisco Live

Leni Selvaggio - June 12, 2012 - 2 Comments

This week thousands of IT professionals will be attending Cisco Live in San Diego so they can learn of innovations that can help them leverage the power of the intelligent network.

We are excited to showcase a variety of solutions that enable financial services firms generate revenue, reduce expense and mitigate risk. Specifically, we will be showing and discussing how Remote Expert for Retail Banking helps enable banks to maximize the efficiency and productivity of financial services advisors so they can capture customer business at first contact and deliver the personalized customer service the customer desires.

Let us give you a demonstration. If you are attending Cisco Live, please visit the Cisco Collaboration Solutions booth in the World of Solutions expo and experience how Cisco Remote Expert can provide a high quality face-to-face meeting experience using Cisco’s enterprise-class video and collaboration product portfolio to address the customers’ desire to get more personal advise from financial service advisors (loans, wealth management, etc.) as they walk into a bank branch. See the customer experience that has banks such as Bank of Montreal’s Paul Dilda, head of branch channel for North America saying “I do see this as a tremendous opportunity for us to punch above our weight and provide tremendous accessibility in places where previously it was not even possible.”

Let’s talk. Cisco experts will be on hand to answer any questions you may have during a Lunch Hour Table Topic conversation focusing on “Remote Expert for Financial Services” on Wednesday, June 13at 11:30 a.m. in Luncheon Hall A&B, located next to the World of Solutions expo. This informal discussion will offer a fresh perspective on how to offer virtual experts to your customers in a life-like, immersive manner regardless of physical location.

Let’s look at the architecture. Come hear industry professional Bil Dry, Cisco solution architect, on Thursday, June 14 at 10:00 a.m. PST in Ballroom 6E, regarding how to plan, build, and deploy the Remote Expert solution. In this session you will learn about the solution architecture and its components, how to scale and integrate the solution into your existing networks, the business process changes needed to maximize your deployment, as well as operational best practices.

With so many planned activities surrounding Cisco Live, don’t forget to follow @CiscoFSI on Twitter, the Cisco-Financial-Services-Industry/135064516527017">Cisco Financial Services Industry Facebook page and the Cisco Financial Services Industry LinkedIn Group to stay abreast of all of the financial services-focused news, events, and information from the show.
