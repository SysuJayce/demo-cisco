Answering Your Top Tech Trend Questions at Cisco Live #CLUS

Giuliano Di Vitantonio - May 19, 2014 - 0 Comments

Our customers join us at Cisco Live conferences around the world to hear how we can help you tackle the most important technology trends. Cloud, security, mobility and programmable networks are all driving different business models and compelling opportunities.

The top questions we hear from you are:

    • What is Cisco’s approach to SDN?
    • How will Cisco help me deliver hybrid cloud services?
    • Can I really deploy applications in hours instead of weeks?
    • Can I get great — but affordable — collaboration technology into every room, on every desk, every pocket?
    • How can IT lead in securing company assets and data?
    • Do I have the right foundation to deliver better mobile experiences to my employees and customers?
    • Which of Cisco’s customers are having success with these technologies?
    • How is Cisco IT grappling with all these challenges?

At Cisco Live US" href="http://www.ciscolive.com/us/" target="_blank">Cisco Live, you not only get the practical, hands-on knowledge that can advance your career. You also get compelling insight and Cisco roadmaps into the ideas, concepts, and emerging trends that are setting the stage for a more connected and collaborative future.

If you want to hear the answers to these questions (and more!) join our Cisco Live technology trend keynotes hosted by Cisco’s senior executives. They will  demonstrate how our services and product portfolio is evolving to help you take advantage of the Internet of Everything, address your top IT concerns, and help you keep pace with business needs, while also protecting your current Cisco investment.

If you haven’t already, register to join us in San Francisco on online.

Can’t make it in person – not a problem, you can tune in online as well as access on demand sessions at your convenience.

From this page, you can find all the sessions and have the ability to register, add to your calendar, and view online.
