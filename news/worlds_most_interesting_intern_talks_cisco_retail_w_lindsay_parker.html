World’s Most Interesting Intern talks Cisco Retail w/ Lindsay Parker

Greg Justice - August 13, 2010 - 42 Comments

Count not him among your friends who will retail your privacies to the world. – Publilius Syrus

When Publilius penned these wise words in the first century B.C., this world was a dark and dreadful place. Planet Earth was flat, not round, and the Prius had yet to be invented. Despite these discrepancies, retail was as central to society as it is today. To commemorate Publilius’ legacy, I sat down with Lindsay Parker, Director and CMO EMM VS, Cisco Retail, to find out what Cisco is doing to improve the 21st-century shopping experience.

Follow me to freedom @GregJustice. Check my other vids!

And don’t miss this week’s featured response, courtesy of the courteous interns at GMDD, who paid for my flight to the Burgh!!
