Sneak Peak: What to Check Out Next Week at Cisco Live 2010

Lindsay Kniffin - June 24, 2010 - 30 Comments

We’re just a few short days away from Cisco Live 2010! Even if you can’t join us in Las Vegas, you can still participate virtually. If you can’t attend virtually, no problem! Starting on June 29, tune in for our live UStream broadcasts that will feature all of the keynotes, executive chat sessions, press conferences and more.

Be sure to follow our Cisco Systems twitter handle for live tweeting during the events. Also, check out our Cisco">Cisco facebook page wall and News@Cisco for updated news and announcements Cisco makes throughout the week!

Below is the UStream broadcast schedule that will be streaming live through our Cisco facebook page.

UStream Broadcast Schedule (In Pacific Standard Time)  

 Tuesday, June 29:

 8:30 am: Cisco Press Conference
10:00 am: Live Keynote with John Chambers
11:45 am: Live Executive Chat Session featuring Carlos Dominguez, SVP, Office of the Chairman and CEO
12:30 pm: Live Panel Discussion – Ipv6 Panel: Experiences with Deploying IPv6
 2:45 pm: Live Partner Super Session: The Impact of Mass Virtualization on Network Management by CA
 4:00 pm: Live Partner Super Session Executive Chat featuring Roger Pilc, Corporate SVP & GM, Products, CA
 4:30 pm: Vibe! Live Wrap-Up of Day 1
 
Wednesday, June 30:

  8:30 am: Cisco Press Conference
10:00 am: Live Keynote with Padmasree Warrior, CTO
11:45 am: Live Executive Chat Session featuring Padmasree Warrior, CTO
12:30 pm: Live Panel Discussion- Public Sector Panel
 2:45 pm: Live Cisco Super Session: Video: What’s Next?
 4:00 pm: Live Cisco Super Session Executive Chat featuring Marthin De Beer, VP, Emerging Technologies Group, Cisco
 4:30 pm: Vibe! Live Wrap-Up of Day 2
 
Thursday, July 1:

10:30 am: Live Guest Keynote: Ben Mezrich, Author
11:45 am: Live Guest Keynote Chat Session: Ben Mezrich
12:30 pm: Live Panel Discussion- Data Center
  2:30 pm: Vibe! Live Wrap of Day 3

See you at Cisco Live!!
