Ready to Roam Las Ramblas (CLEUR 2018)

Jodie Perry - January 24, 2018 - 3 Comments

¡Hola familia de Cisco!

We are all super excited to be attending Cisco Live Barcelona next week, talking to attendees from over 30 countries about what matters to them and how the IT Management Program will help them navigate their own digital transformations.

 

Meet the Team:

¡Hola Barcelona! As a multimedia content producer within IT at Cisco, I’m looking forward to telling visual stories around the attendee experience at Cisco Live! It’s an event that gets bigger and better every year, offering new immersive technologies for attendees to learn and play with, and continues to build upon our strong customer relationships. Barcelona is a city of innovation, a smart city that embraces technology and utilizes it for the public good. We’re excited to share wonderful memories with all who join us onsite and virtually. If you’re attending the IT Management Program, please come over to meet us! We’d love to hear what you’re looking forward to learning and if we can answer any questions you may have. See you soon!

~Alex Felker

 

Hey there! I am the regional IT lead for the Middle Eastern region working from Dubai. This is my first Cisco Live, which is very exciting mostly because Cisco Live is a platform that brings all sorts of people from around the world to collaborate with Cisco as its foundation, and experiencing that live is going to be incredible. #BringIT on!

~Ahmet Emre Aribulan

 

Hi Everyone! I’m excited to be representing Cisco’s Global Infrastructure Services, and the Network Services Team at Cisco Live Barcelona for #CiscoITM! I’ve been with Cisco since 2012 and I am a Senior Network Engineer responsible for the remote access services within Cisco IT. I cannot wait to meet you all and hear your thoughts on how Cisco is enabling your #DigitalTransformation!

~Damien Stenning

 

Hello! Greeting from Krakow, Poland! I can’t wait for Cisco Live Barcelona to arrive so we can meet and network with all of the IT Management Program presenters and participants! I joined Cisco last year as an IT Project Manager supporting the EMEAR region and I am really excited for this new Cisco experience. See you in Barcelona, let’s #BringIT on!

~Dorota Bek

 

Hi All, I am very excited to be going to Cisco Live Barcelona, my very first Cisco Live. I’m based out of Cisco’s Bedfont Lakes office in the UK, where I am a Business Analyst for the eStore team; however, next week I am looking forward to the Spanish sun! I am thrilled to have the opportunity to talk to our customers about what our innovations mean to them and hear from our leaders on what’s next for the company and the industry. #WeAreCisco #BestPlaceToWork

~Jodie Perry
