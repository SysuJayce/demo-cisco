Inside Cisco IT

Cisco wins at recent CITE awards

Evan Parthenis - May 14, 2014 - 0 Comments

We Won!! I’m pleased to announce the Cisco IT Any Device team beat 50 companies to win the “Best Business Computing Environment” at the 2014 IDG CITE awards in San Francisco. The Any Device team manage, secure, and support over 196,000 desktops, laptops, tablets and smartphones including 70,000 BYO devices.

Cisco won the award for providing its workforce more choice in the devices they use, and more flexibility in where they can use them. The awards were judged by a panel of industry experts based on the range of hardware options offered; ease of use across allowable devices; application design to ensure quick access; and the role that UX objectives played in solution design.

In accepting the award, Kelly Conway, IT Senior Manager at Cisco said “Our goal with Any Device was to allow greater choice in devices and deliver a supreme user experience while protecting our customer and corporate data. The vast majority of Cisco workers use a computer and smartphone to access company services, with over 20 percent using more than two devices, and the diversity of those devices is growing exponentially.”

As an employee at Cisco you can choose from one of eight laptops or desktops running Windows, Linux, or OS X. Plus, every three years you can choose a brand new device.
